Text Alignment Page

Useful Knowledge:

<h1>,<h2>,<h3>, and <h4> are headers where the lower the corresponding number, the bigger the font. This is kind of a short cut for changing text size.


How to align text:


Inside a body tag, type in: style="text-align:(choose either center, left or right)". This simple formatting style is very important in design.

Ex: <h3 style="text-align:center">I'm a header!</h3>
